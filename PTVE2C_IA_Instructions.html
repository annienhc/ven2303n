How to Complete This Exercise

About This Exercise

This exercise helps you understand how to transform brief work plan items into complete, professional instructions. You'll practice adding necessary connecting words, articles, and phrases.

1

Review the Complete Work Plan

First, familiarize yourself with the entire work plan table to understand the context.

2

View the Row Being Tested

Each question highlights the specific row from the work plan that you'll work with.

3

Fill in the Blanks

Type appropriate words into the blank spaces to create complete, grammatically correct sentences.

4

Check Your Work

Click "Check My Work" to see if your answers are correct and receive feedback.

Tip: Think about action verbs (submit, send), modals (should), articles (a, an, the) and prepositions (such as, to, for) that make the sentence flow naturally.

Listening & Arrange

Listen and arrange words

Content

How to Complete This Exercise

Follow the steps below to master listening and sentence construction

About This Exercise

This interactive listening exercise helps you practice understanding workplace instructions and arranging words in the correct order. Each sentence uses vocabulary from your Fill in the Blanks and Proofreading exercises.

1

Listen to Each Sentence

Click the speaker icon to hear the complete sentence. You can replay it as many times as you need.

2

Drag and Drop Words

Drag the shuffled words from the word bank into the answer area in the correct order.

3

Check Your Answer

Click "Check Answer" for each sentence to see if you've arranged the words correctly.

4

Complete All Sentences

Work through all 6 sentences on the page to finish the exercise.

Tip: Listen carefully to the word order and punctuation. Pay attention to articles, prepositions, and linking words.
